Create your own virtual worlds

Each project always start with a story. Back in 2020 we imagined virtual places where meeting and interacting would be as easy as saying 'hello' to someone in the street.

Two years later we are proud to have delivered a platform that will let you imagine, build & monetize your own virtual worlds. And interconnect with thousand others!

virtual worls

open source
we are transparent with everything we do. No need to believe us, go check by yourself.
really decentralized
be free in your hosting! self-hosted or with us, the experience will be the same when jumping from a world to another
blockchain agnostic
connect your wallets, use your NFTs, define who you will let in your world by picking up a token or a NFT collection
web2 bridge
integrate all your current digital assets (e-commerce, CRM, ERP...) directly in your virtual worlds and surprise your stakeholders
social interactions
meet, exchange, collaborate while having fun with thousand of users in other worlds
monetize
trade your creations, worlds, maps, assets, parcels, accessories... using our utility token
learn
share your contents & bring knowledge to a wider community
play
imagine new ways of playing and earning!
